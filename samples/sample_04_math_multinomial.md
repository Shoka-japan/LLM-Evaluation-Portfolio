# Sample 04: Math Multinomial

## Prompt

What is the coefficient of a²b²c² in (a + b + c)^6?

## Response A

The coefficient is 90 because 6! / (2!2!2!) = 90.

## Response B

The coefficient is 720 because 6! = 720.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A
