# Sample 28: Math Units

## Prompt

Convert 2.5 kilometers to meters.

## Response A

2.5 kilometers is 2500 meters.

## Response B

2.5 kilometers is 250 meters.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A correctly uses 1 km = 1000 m. Response B is off by a factor of 10.
