# Sample 07: Summarization

## Prompt

Summarize this idea: Regular exercise improves physical health, mental health, and long-term disease prevention.

## Response A

Regular exercise supports physical and mental health and helps reduce long-term disease risk.

## Response B

Exercise is only useful for building muscle.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A captures all key points concisely. Response B omits mental health and prevention and adds a misleading limitation.
