# Sample 21: Reasoning Logic

## Prompt

If all mammals are animals and all dogs are mammals, are all dogs animals?

## Response A

Yes. Since dogs are mammals and mammals are animals, dogs are animals.

## Response B

No, because dogs are different from other animals.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

|---|---:|---:|

| Clarity | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A correctly applies syllogistic reasoning. Response B contradicts the logical relationship.
