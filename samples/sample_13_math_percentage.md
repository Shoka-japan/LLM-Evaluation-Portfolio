# Sample 13: Math Percentage

## Prompt

A price increases from 2000 yen to 2500 yen. What is the percentage increase?

## Response A

The increase is 500 yen, so 500 / 2000 = 0.25, or 25%.

## Response B

The increase is 20% because 500 / 2500 = 0.20.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A uses the original price as the denominator. Response B uses the final price and gives the wrong percentage increase.
