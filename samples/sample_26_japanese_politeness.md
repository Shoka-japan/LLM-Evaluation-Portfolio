# Sample 26: Japanese Politeness

## Prompt

友達ではなく先生に使うべき表現を選んでください。

## Response A

お忙しいところ恐れ入りますが、ご確認いただけますでしょうか。

## Response B

ちょっと見てくれる？

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A is polite and suitable for a teacher. Response B is casual and inappropriate for the requested context.
