# Sample 17: Long Form Quality

## Prompt

Give two tips for improving concentration while studying.

## Response A

Set a clear goal for each study session and remove distractions such as phone notifications.

## Response B

Just try harder and study all day.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A gives specific and practical tips. Response B is vague and potentially unhealthy.
