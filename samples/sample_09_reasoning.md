# Sample 09: Reasoning

## Prompt

A bat and a ball cost $1.10 total. The bat costs $1 more than the ball. How much is the ball?

## Response A

The ball costs $0.05. If the ball is x, the bat is x + 1.00, so 2x + 1.00 = 1.10 and x = 0.05.

## Response B

The ball costs $0.10 because $1.10 - $1.00 = $0.10.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A uses correct algebra. Response B is the common intuitive mistake and does not satisfy the condition.
