# Sample 11: Japanese Translation Formality

## Prompt

Translate naturally into Japanese: Could you please send me the document by tomorrow?

## Response A

明日までにその書類を送っていただけますでしょうか。

## Response B

あなたは明日までに文書を送ることができますか。

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A is natural and polite Japanese. Response B is understandable but unnatural and less appropriate.
