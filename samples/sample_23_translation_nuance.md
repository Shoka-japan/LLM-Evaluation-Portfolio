# Sample 23: Translation Nuance

## Prompt

Translate into English: 彼は来るかもしれません。

## Response A

He might come.

## Response B

He must come.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 2 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 2 |
| Completeness | 5 | 2 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Reasoning / Quality | 5 | 2 |

## Explanation

Response A preserves uncertainty. Response B incorrectly changes the meaning to obligation or certainty.
