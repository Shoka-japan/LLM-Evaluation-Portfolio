# Sample 03: Translation Quality

## Prompt

Translate the following Japanese sentence into natural English:

今日はいい天気ですね。

## Response A

The weather is nice today.

## Response B

Today weather good.

## Evaluation

**Winner:** Response A

## Scores

| Criterion | Response A | Response B |
|---|---:|---:|
| Instruction Following | 5 | 3 |
| Accuracy | 5 | 3 |
| Completeness | 5 | 3 |
| Clarity | 5 | 2 |
| Naturalness | 5 | 1 |

## Explanation

Response A accurately translates the meaning of the Japanese sentence into natural English. It is grammatically correct and sounds natural.

Response B roughly conveys the meaning, but it is ungrammatical and unnatural. It would not be acceptable for a high-quality translation task.
